Investing

What I’m excited about...

  • Tools that fundamentally change the way we work — not strictly enterprise SaaS or productivity. Things that are genuinely useful to people in their jobs. Industry doesn’t really matter to me. Creative tools or things that encourage social mobility, are a bonus.
  • People with a strong vision, or excitement around reinvention. I gravitate towards ideas that feel smart or novel and people I believe have a high calibre for it. I like things that make me say, “aha!” and people who have the the intuition and taste to make these things.
  • Supporting founders from underrepresented or non-traditional backgrounds. I want more of you in the world, and I’m willing to put my money there.
